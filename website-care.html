Fair questions.

What if you didn't build my website?

That's fine. I'll do a health check first so we both know what shape it's in, then take it from there. Most sites I care for were built by someone else.

What counts as a "small edit"?

Swapping photos, updating hours or prices, adding a testimonial, tweaking text. If something is bigger, I'll tell you before I start and quote it plainly. No surprise invoices, ever.

What happens if my site goes down?

I usually know before you do. Monitoring alerts me the moment something's wrong, and getting you back online becomes my first priority.

Can I cancel?

Any time, no penalty. Your site, your backups, and your accounts are yours. I'll hand everything over cleanly if you ever move on.
